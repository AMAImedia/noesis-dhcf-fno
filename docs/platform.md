# The NOESIS Platform

NOESIS by amaimedia is a full creative-media platform. Dubbing is the flagship; the same
foundation powers voice, video, music, and image — orchestrated through one assistant.

## Surfaces

### 🎬 Dubbing — *flagship*
Multi-speaker, lip-synced video dubbing in 600+ languages, each actor's voice preserved.
→ [dubbing.md](dubbing.md)

### 🗣️ Voice
Clone a voice from a 5–30 second reference and synthesize **48 kHz** speech. Multilingual —
the voice's timbre is preserved across languages. Powers dubbing and standalone narration,
dictation, and audiobook workflows.

### ✂️ Video edit
Turn one long video into many vertical **Shorts / Reels / TikTok (9:16)** clips —
automatic hook detection, AI-written titles and descriptions, and burned-in subtitles.

### 🎵 Music
Generate original, royalty-clear music from a text prompt — for backing tracks, intros,
and ambience inside dubbed or edited video.

### 🖼️ Image
Generate images from a text prompt for thumbnails, posters, and key art.

### 💬 Assistant
A conversational layer that plans and runs the surfaces above — "dub this into Spanish,"
"cut 10 shorts," "make a thumbnail" — and tracks each job to completion.

## One pipeline, many outputs

These surfaces share a common job model (submit → poll → retrieve) and a common quality
philosophy: **quality over speed, never skip the gates.** Every output is checked before
it's returned.

## Principles

- **Quality bar set for distribution**, not demos.
- **Identity-preserving** — your voice, your actors, your brand.
- **Global by default** — 600+ languages.
- **Proprietary engines** — the *what* is documented here; the *how* stays in-house.
