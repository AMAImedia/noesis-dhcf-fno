# Features

Everything on [amaimedia.com](https://amaimedia.com), grouped. **Dubbing is the flagship;**
the rest share the same engines and **one token pool** — no per-tool subscriptions.

## 🎬 Dubbing — *flagship*
Dub film, series, and online video into **201 languages** at studio quality.
- Per-actor **voice cloning** — each speaker keeps their own voice
- **Multi-speaker** auto-separation (up to 20 per title)
- **±50 ms** lip-sync timing · **48 kHz** audio · music & ambience preserved
- **C2PA-signed** output (provenance)
→ [/dubbing](https://amaimedia.com/dubbing) · deep dive: [dubbing.md](dubbing.md)
